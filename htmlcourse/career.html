Career

She is a nurse at Cooperative For Human Services, a non-profit that helps people with developmental disabilities manage their healthcare. Lisa is also working as a tutor at The Coder School helping kids make computer games and designing websites using Scratch, HTML, CSS and Javascript. She is working on becoming a web developer in the healthcare industry. Some of the applications that she would love to work on are those related to exercise and nutrition.
